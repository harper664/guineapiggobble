Daily diet basics

  • Hay always available. Roughly 80% of the diet.
  • About 1 cup of fresh veg per pig per day, mostly leafy greens.
  • A vitamin C source daily. Guinea pigs cannot make their own.
  • Around 1/8 cup of plain pellets daily.
  • Introduce any new food slowly and in small amounts.

A quick guide, not veterinary advice. For health concerns or foods not listed, check with an exotic vet.
